Chocalate Milkshake Recipe

Whipping cream makes this chocolate milkshake recipe creamy and light as air. Drizzle with chocolate syrup after you pour in the cream for an even more sensational chocolaty treat.

Ingredients
  1. 2 cups premium quality chocolate ice cream
  2. 2 (1.65 ounce) bars dark chocolate candy bars, diced
  3. ½ cup whipped cream
  4. 1 tablespoon chocolate shavings, for garnish
  5. ¼ cup whipped cream
Steps
  1. Combine ice cream, diced chocolate, and 1/2 cup whipped cream in a blender. Blend until smooth.
  2. Pour into 2 glasses; garnish with chocolate shavings and 1/4 cup whipped cream.
